Task 2: Foundation Model Timeline

2018 – BERT (Google)

Introduced bidirectional understanding of natural language.

2018 – GPT-1 (OpenAI)

First transformer-based generative language model.

2019 – GPT-2

Improved text coherence and long-form generation.

2020 – GPT-3

Few-shot learning with large-scale parameters.

2022 – PaLM (Google)

Enhanced reasoning and multilingual capabilities.

2023 – GPT-4

Multimodal understanding and advanced reasoning.

2024 – LLaMA (Meta)

Efficient and open-source foundation model.

2025 – GPT-5

Next-generation AI with improved autonomy and intelligence.
